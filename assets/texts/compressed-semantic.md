### Background

Steering can be very subtle and condense more then just the high level feature we intended to represent. Encountered example: steering a LLM away from information retrieval, it *confabulate* the bio the unfamous people (desired outcome), but additionaly it made all the people being influencers (unexpectedly precise confabulation).

In addition we observe that lengthy prompt can reach same representation as short prompts.
